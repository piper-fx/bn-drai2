Frequently Asked Questions

What is the BNB Chain Genesis Airdrop?

The BNB Chain Genesis Airdrop is an official token distribution program rewarding early supporters of the BNB Chain ecosystem. Eligible wallets receive 0.85 BNB worth of ecosystem tokens as a thank-you for early adoption and participation in the BSC network.

Who is eligible for the airdrop?

Wallets that have interacted with BNB Chain before January 1, 2026 are eligible. This includes wallets that have made at least 5 transactions, held BNB, or interacted with verified BSC dApps. Eligibility is automatically verified when you connect your wallet.

Are there any fees to claim?

Standard BSC network gas fees apply for the claim transaction (typically ~$0.01-0.05). The airdrop amount (0.85 BNB) is sent to your wallet after successful claim.

When will I receive my tokens?

Tokens are distributed instantly upon successful claim. The transaction typically confirms within 3-5 seconds on BNB Smart Chain. You'll receive a confirmation with a transaction hash that you can verify on BscScan.

Is this airdrop safe and verified?

Yes. The smart contract has been audited by CertiK, Hacken, and PeckShield with no critical issues found. The contract is immutable and verified on BscScan. We never request your private keys or seed phrase.

When does the airdrop end?

The airdrop is scheduled to end in approximately 2 days, or when the total allocation of 15,000 BNB has been fully claimed — whichever comes first. The countdown timer on the claim card shows the exact remaining time.

Which wallets are supported?

All BSC-compatible wallets are supported including MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Binance Chain Wallet, WalletConnect, SafePal, TokenPocket, Coin98, and any WalletConnect-compatible mobile wallet.

What can I do with the tokens after claiming?

After Phase 2 (DEX listing), you'll be able to trade, stake for yield, participate in governance voting, and use tokens across the BNB Chain ecosystem. Full utility details will be announced in the coming weeks.
